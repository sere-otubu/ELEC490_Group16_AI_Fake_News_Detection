Title: Behavioural and social drivers of COVID-19 vaccination in Papua New Guinea: A cross-sectional study.

Abstract: Papua New Guinea (PNG) has one of the world's lowest COVID-19 vaccination rates, despite high awareness of the disease. Understanding the behavioural and social drivers of vaccine acceptance is essential for informing context-appropriate strategies to improve uptake. This study is the first in PNG to systematically apply the WHO Behavioural and Social Drivers (BeSD) framework to examine factors associated with COVID-19 vaccination status.
