Title: Attenuated viral strains of priority pathogens for potential use in controlled human infection model studies: A scoping review.

Abstract: There are several known pathogens and families identified as high risk for pandemic potential. It is essential to study these pathogens and develop medical countermeasures to mitigate disease prior to potential pandemics. Controlled human infection models (CHIMs) using attenuated viral strains may offer an efficient and safe way to do this.
