Title: Cost-effectiveness of female-only and gender-neutral HPV vaccination strategies in Japan.

Abstract: This study evaluated the cost-effectiveness of female-only vaccination (FOV) and gender-neutral vaccination strategies (GNV) using 4-valent (4vHPV) and 9-valent HPV (9vHPV) vaccines among adolescents aged 12-16 years in Japan. It provides the first comprehensive assessment of all feasible vaccine-population combinations using a unified dynamic transmission model.
