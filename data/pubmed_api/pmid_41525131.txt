Title: A new tool for assessing tobacco use among health profession students: an updated version of the Global Health Professions Student Survey (GHPSS).

Abstract: The aim of this study is to validate a new tool, based on the framework of the GHPSS, including questions on the use of all tobacco- and nicotine-based products currently available on the market (cigarettes, electronic cigarettes, and heated tobacco products), providing an updated version of the GHPSS tool.
