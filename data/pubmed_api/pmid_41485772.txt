Title: Risk of Hospitalized COVID-19 in COPD: Single-Inhaler Triple Versus Dual Bronchodilator Therapy.

Abstract: Our objective was to estimate the effect of initiating an inhaled corticosteroids-containing single-inhaler triple agent (ICS-LABA-LAMA) compared with a single-inhaler LABA-LAMA dual bronchodilator in patients with COPD on the risk of severe COVID-19 prior to the roll-out of vaccines.
