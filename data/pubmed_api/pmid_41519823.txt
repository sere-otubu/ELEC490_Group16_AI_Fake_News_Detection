Title: Systematic review protocol: evaluation of candidate platforms and vaccines for emerging and re-emerging viral threats.

Abstract: Emerging viral threats such as coronavirus, influenza, Lassa fever, Mpox, and Nipah virus continue to pose significant global health challenges. The development and deployment of effective vaccines are essential for outbreak control and pandemic preparedness. This protocol describes a systematic review that will synthesize evidence on vaccine candidates targeting high-priority viral threats and major vaccine platforms.
