Title: Adverse events following Synflorix vaccination reported to the Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System (VAERS), 2010-2024.

Abstract: Synflorix is a conjugate vaccine targeting Streptococcus pneumoniae, effectively reducing pneumonia and invasive pneumococcal disease in children under five. To evaluate its safety profile, this study analyzed adverse event (AE) reports associated with Synflorix vaccination submitted to the Vaccine Adverse Event Reporting System (VAERS) from 2010 to 2024.
