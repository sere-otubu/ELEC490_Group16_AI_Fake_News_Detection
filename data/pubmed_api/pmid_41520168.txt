Title: Greyscale, color Doppler, and oscillometric macroangiopathy indices in patients with autoinflammatory syndromes: the INFLAMMACARD cohort.

Abstract: To evaluate for the first time a combination of novel colour Doppler ultrasound (CDUS), greyscale (GSUS), and oscillometric indices of angiopathy in patients with autoinflammatory syndromes (AIS). Furthermore, to explore the associations between these markers and patient- and disease-related characteristics, as well as traditional cardiovascular (CV) risk factors.
