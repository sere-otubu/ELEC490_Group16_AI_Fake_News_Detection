Title: Differences in Discrimination, Violence, Mental Health, and Substance Use Outcomes by Cancer History Among LGBTQ+ Individuals in North Carolina.

Abstract: Health inequities among lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and other sexual and gender minority populations (LGBTQ+) are driven by anti-LGBTQ+ stigma and discrimination. However, there is limited research on the manifestations of stigma and discrimination among LGBTQ+ cancer survivors.
