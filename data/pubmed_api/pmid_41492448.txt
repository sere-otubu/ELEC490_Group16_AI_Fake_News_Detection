Title: Safety of the BBIBP-CorV COVID-19 Vaccine in Relation to Adverse Clinical Outcomes in Newborns: A Prospective Cohort Study.

Abstract: The aim of this study is to evaluate the safety of preconception inoculation with an inactivated SARS-CoV-2 vaccine with respect to adverse birth outcomes in newborns, to elucidate the potential association between inactivated COVID-19 vaccination and birth outcomes, and to provide scientific evidence to inform future vaccine policy development.
