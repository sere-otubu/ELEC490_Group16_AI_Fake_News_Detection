Title: Global Prevalence of Combustible Cigarettes and Noncombustible Nicotine or Tobacco Products Use Among Adolescents, 2014-2021.

Abstract: Understanding global patterns of adolescent noncombustible nicotine or tobacco product (NNTP) and combustible cigarette (CC) use is vital due to rising prevalence and long-term health risks, but comparative international analyses are lacking. Thus, we aimed to assess smoking prevalence and trends among adolescents in 57 countries and territories and to identify factors associated with NNTP and CC use.
