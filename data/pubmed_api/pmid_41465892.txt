Title: Esophageal bottle cap impaction in adult: A case report.

Abstract: Adult esophageal foreign-body impaction is typically food-related, whereas impaction of large non-food objects is rare and usually associated with structural abnormalities or altered mental status. We present an unusual case of an alert adult with chronic ethanol-nicotine co-use who remained asymptomatic for 3 hours after ingesting a beer bottle cap, highlighting the potential for substance-related impairment of protective pharyngeal reflexes.
