    QUESTION: Chemoradiation instead of surgery to treat mid and low rectal tumors: is it safe?
    CONTEXT: The main treatment for rectal carcinoma is surgery. Preoperative chemoradiation (CRT) is advocated to reduce local recurrence and improve resection of mid and low tethered rectal tumors.
    ANSWER: Exclusive CRT approach is not safe to treat patients with low infiltrative rectal carcinoma.
