
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 10430303)
    QUESTION: Does laparoscopic cholecystectomy influence peri-sinusoidal cell activity?
    CONTEXT: To investigate the influence of laparoscopic procedures on perisinusoidal cell function.
    ANSWER: Laparoscopic procedures caused detectable damage to Kupffer and endothelial cells as reflected by elevation of post-operative HA serum levels. The damage to the liver hepatocytes and perisinusoidal cells has no clinical significance and the laparoscopic procedure itself is the probable cause of the Kupffer and endothelial cells damage, while other mechanisms caused damage to the hepatocytes.
