SEARCH_TOPIC: Speech therapy
TITLE: The effect of tele-speech therapy on treatment of stuttering.
PMID: 32299263
SOURCE_URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/32299263/
TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
The aim of this study was to evaluate the effect of tele-speech therapy on the recovery of patients with a stutter. This research was an interventional study conducted on the patients visiting two rehabilitation centres. In this study, patients with a stutter participated in the tele-speech therapy sessions <i>via</i> Skype. Stuttering Severity Instrument-Fourth Edition was used to determine the severity of stutter before and after the intervention. The results showed a significant difference in the mean scores of stuttering severity before and after tele-speech therapy (<i>p</i> = 0.001). Our findings showed a mean reduction of 13.8% in stuttering scores. More than half of the patients (56.6%) reported a "high" and "very high" satisfaction with this treatment method. Tele-speech therapy is an effective way for the treatment of stuttering patients irrespective of their age, gender, and educational background. This method can improve patient's speech and reduce stuttering.
