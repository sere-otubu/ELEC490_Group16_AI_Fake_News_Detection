
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 22540518)
    QUESTION: Is micro-computed tomography reliable to determine the microstructure of the maxillary alveolar bone?
    CONTEXT: To analyze the reliability of micro-computed tomography (micro-CT) to assess bone density and the microstructure of the maxillary bones at the alveolar process in human clinics by direct comparison with conventional stereologic-based histomorphometry.
    ANSWER: BV/TV assessed by micro-CT correlates with the percentage of bone assessed by conventional stereologic-based histomorphometry. Micro-CT is a reliable technique to determine the bone density and the microstructure of the maxillary alveolar bone at the site of dental implant insertion.
