
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 23517744)
    QUESTION: Is solitary kidney really more resistant to ischemia?
    CONTEXT: To our knowledge there are no evidence-based medicine data to date to critically judge the vulnerability of a solitary kidney to warm ischemia compared to paired kidneys.
    ANSWER: Solitary kidney in a canine model is more resistant to ischemia than paired kidneys based on radiological, pathological and genetic evidence.
