
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 21190419)
    QUESTION: Does nuchal translucency thickness in the first trimester predict GDM onset during pregnancy?
    CONTEXT: This study was planned to evaluate whether increased nuchal translucency (NT) thickness in the first trimester of gestation can be related to onset of gestational diabetes mellitus (GDM) during pregnancy.
    ANSWER: NT thickness does not show a significative increase in those women who subsequently develop GDM. Therefore, NT assessment does not prove to be an useful ultrasound parameter for predicting GDM onset during pregnancy.
