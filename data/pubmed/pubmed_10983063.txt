TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
Patients in hospitals receive 10,000 transfusions every day due to blood disorders, trauma and disease. Nurses play a significant role in the safe administration of blood and its products. This guide describes the nursing care of the patient before, during and after a blood transfusion.
