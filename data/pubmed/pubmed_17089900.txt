    QUESTION: Does Paget's disease exist in India?
    CONTEXT: Paget's disease of bone has been described as a few case reports from India. The aim of the present study is to document the existence of Paget's disease (PD) in India.
    ANSWER: This illustrates that Paget's disease does exist in India and a high index of suspicion is required to clinch the diagnosis.
