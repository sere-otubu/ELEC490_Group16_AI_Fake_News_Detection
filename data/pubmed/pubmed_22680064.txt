
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 22680064)
    QUESTION: Can third trimester ultrasound predict the presentation of the first twin at delivery?
    CONTEXT: To determine the ability of early sonogram to predict the presentation of twin A at birth.
    ANSWER: The presentation of the first twin at delivery can be determined by sonogram by the 32nd week of gestation in over 90% of twin pregnancies.
