
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 27288618)
    QUESTION: Is inhaled prophylactic heparin useful for prevention and Management of Pneumonia in ventilated ICU patients?
    CONTEXT: To determine whether prophylactic inhaled heparin is effective for the prevention and treatment of pneumonia patients receiving mechanical ventilation (MV) in the intensive care unit.
    ANSWER: Nebulized heparin cannot be recommended for prophylaxis against VAP or to hasten recovery from pneumonia in patients receiving MV.
