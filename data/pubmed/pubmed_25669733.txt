
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 25669733)
    QUESTION: Can distal ureteral diameter predict reflux resolution after endoscopic injection?
    CONTEXT: To test the predictive value of distal ureteral diameter (UD) on reflux resolution after endoscopic injection in children with primary vesicoureteral reflux (VUR).
    ANSWER: UDR provides an objective measurement of VUR and appears as a predictive tool of success after endoscopic injection.
