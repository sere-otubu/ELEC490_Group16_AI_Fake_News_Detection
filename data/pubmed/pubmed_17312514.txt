
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 17312514)
    QUESTION: Does quilting suture prevent seroma in abdominoplasty?
    CONTEXT: Seroma is the most frequent complication in abdominoplasty. Some patients are more prone to develop this complication. Ultrasound is a well-known method with which to diagnose seroma in the abdominal wall. The purpose of this study was to verify the efficacy of the use of quilting suture to prevent seroma.
    ANSWER: The quilting suture seems to be an efficient technique with which to prevent seroma formation.
