    QUESTION: Does oxybutynin hydrochloride cause arrhythmia in children with bladder dysfunction?
    CONTEXT: This study represents a subset of a complete data set, considering only those children aged admitted to the Pediatric Surgery and Pediatric Nephrology Clinics during the period January 2011 to July 2012.
    ANSWER: For this reason, children using such drugs should be closely monitored for cardiac arrhythmia.
