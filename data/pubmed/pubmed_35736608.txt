SEARCH_TOPIC: HPV vaccine
TITLE: HPV-Associated Cancer Trends Since Vaccine Initiation.
PMID: 35736608
SOURCE_URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/35736608/
TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
Decreasing cervical cancer rates may be associated with human papillomavirus (HPV) vaccine use.It may be too early to evaluate the association between HPV vaccine use and trends in oropharyngeal cancer in men.
