    QUESTION: Is Acupuncture Efficacious for Treating Phonotraumatic Vocal Pathologies?
    CONTEXT: To investigate the effectiveness of acupuncture in treating phonotraumatic vocal fold lesions.STUDY DESIGN/
    ANSWER: The findings showed that acupuncture of voice-related acupoints could bring about improvement in vocal function and healing of vocal fold lesions.
