TITLE: Varicose Veins: Approach, Assessment, and Management to the Patient with Chronic Venous Disease.
PMID: 37541715
SOURCE_URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37541715/
TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
Varicose veins are tortuous and dilated veins commonly seen in chronic venous disease. This article will review chronic venous disease, including its differential diagnosis, workup, and treatment.
