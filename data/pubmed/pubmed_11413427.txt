    QUESTION: Does transverse apex coincide with coronal apex levels (regional or global) in adolescent idiopathic scoliosis?
    CONTEXT: Cross-sectional.
    ANSWER: This study demonstrated that the regional or global apex of a given curve is the most rotated level in only a minority of the curves. The most rotated level may be as far as two levels from the global apex and one level from the regional apex.
