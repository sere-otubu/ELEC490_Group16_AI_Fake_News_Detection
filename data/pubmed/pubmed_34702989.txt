SEARCH_TOPIC: Red blood cells
TITLE: Red blood cells join the ranks as immune sentinels.
PMID: 34702989
SOURCE_URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/34702989/
TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
New research shows that red blood cells express surface TLR9 that binds cell-free DNA, leading to erythrophagocytosis and innate immune activation.
