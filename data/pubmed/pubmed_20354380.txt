
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 20354380)
    QUESTION: Do women residents delay childbearing due to perceived career threats?
    CONTEXT: To assess gender differences among residents regarding their plans to have children during residency and determine the most influential reasons for these differences.
    ANSWER: Women residents intentionally postpone pregnancy because of perceived threats to their careers. Medical educators should be aware of these findings when counseling female trainees.
