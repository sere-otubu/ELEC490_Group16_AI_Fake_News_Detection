    QUESTION: Does the manic/mixed episode distinction in bipolar disorder patients run true over time?
    CONTEXT: The authors sought to determine whether the manic/mixed episode distinction in patients with bipolar disorder runs true over time.
    ANSWER: These data suggest a pattern of diagnostic stability over time for manic and mixed episodes in patients with bipolar disorder. Careful prospective studies of this issue are needed.
