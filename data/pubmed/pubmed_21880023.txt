
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 21880023)
    QUESTION: Does exercise during pregnancy prevent postnatal depression?
    CONTEXT: To study whether exercise during pregnancy reduces the risk of postnatal depression.
    ANSWER: We did not find a lower prevalence of high EPDS scores among women randomized to regular exercise during pregnancy compared with the control group. However, a subgroup of women in the intervention group who did not exercise regularly prior to pregnancy had a reduced risk of postnatal depression.
