SEARCH_TOPIC: Osteoarthritis
TITLE: Treatment options for symptomatic knee osteoarthritis in adults.
PMID: 37884044
SOURCE_URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/37884044/
TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
Knee osteoarthritis is a common medical condition in adults, especially among older adults. The incidence and prevalence of knee osteoarthritis are increasing, and many healthcare providers manage patients with symptomatic presentations. This article reviews the most common nonsurgical and surgical treatment options for knee osteoarthritis, emphasizing evidence-based and practical therapies.
