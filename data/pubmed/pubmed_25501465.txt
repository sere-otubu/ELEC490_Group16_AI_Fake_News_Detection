
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 25501465)
    QUESTION: Evaluation of pediatric VCUG at an academic children's hospital: is the radiographic scout image necessary?
    CONTEXT: There is heterogeneity in how pediatric voiding cystourethrography (VCUG) is performed. Some institutions, including our own, obtain a radiographic scout image prior to contrast agent instillation.
    ANSWER: Although the estimated effective dose is low for VCUG radiographic scout images, this step did not augment VCUG interpretation or contribute management-changing information. This step should be omitted or substituted to further reduce dose in pediatric VCUG.
