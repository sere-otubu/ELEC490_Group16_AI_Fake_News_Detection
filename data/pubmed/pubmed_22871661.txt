TITLE: Clinical Inquiry. Multivitamins for healthy children: what are the true benefits?
PMID: 22871661
SOURCE_URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/22871661/
TYPE: PubMed Medical Abstract

ABSTRACT:
The benefits appear to be limited. It's doubtful that multivitamin with mineral (MVM) supplementation improves IQ in healthy, low-risk children.
