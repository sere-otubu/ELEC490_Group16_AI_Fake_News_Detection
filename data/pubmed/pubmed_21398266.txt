
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 21398266)
    QUESTION: Do symptoms matter when considering patients for phase I clinical trials?
    CONTEXT: Older adults (OA) with advanced cancer (AC) undergoing phase I clinical trials (PICT) have poor prognosis. There are no studies which describe symptoms experienced by OA.
    ANSWER: Older adults enrolled in PICT with more symptoms may sacrifice QOL for experimental treatment.
