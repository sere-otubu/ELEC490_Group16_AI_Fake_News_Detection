
    SOURCE: PubMed QA (ID: 11833948)
    QUESTION: Does a delay in transfer to a rehabilitation unit for older people affect outcome after fracture of the proximal femur?
    CONTEXT: To detemine the relationship between delay in transfer to rehabilitation wards and outcome for patients aged over 75 years with fracture of the proximal femur.
    ANSWER: Delay in transfer to a rehabilitation ward was associated with a disproportionate increase in total hospital length of stay for patients aged over 75 with fracture of the proximal femur.
